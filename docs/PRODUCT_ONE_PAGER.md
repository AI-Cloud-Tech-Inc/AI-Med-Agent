# Product One-Pager (Enterprise-Ready)

## Product Name

Autonomous Agentic AI Med Agent

## What It Is

A HIPAA-compliant, autonomous AI platform that listens (with consent) to doctor–patient conversations, generates structured clinical documentation in real time, and streamlines healthcare and insurance workflows—while keeping doctors in full control.

## Key Capabilities

- Real-time medical speech-to-notes automation.
- Multi-agent orchestration for diagnosis, documentation, follow-up.
- Doctor-approved clinical decision support.
- Secure patient read-only portal.
- Patient access to insurance status & claims.
- Insurance & billing workflow automation.
- Full audit logs and governance.
- Patient-initiated emergency escalation with clinician notification.

## Who It’s For

- Hospitals & clinics.
- Physicians & care teams.
- Healthcare enterprises.
- Insurance-integrated providers.

## Business Impact

- Reduces clinician documentation time by 60–70%.
- Improves billing accuracy & claim success.
- Enhances patient transparency & trust.
- Ensures regulatory compliance at scale.
